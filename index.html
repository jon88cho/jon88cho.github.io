Basketball Research Project

Something interesting that I noticed was how players' defensive ability are often judged by actions like blocks and steals. I wanted to explore a correlation between numbers and tangible ability.

I scraped data from Basketball Reference and NBA.com, and asked the question How do Defensive Metrics correlate with Defensive All Team Nominations?

- Performed statistical analyses such as Chi-Squared Testing and confidence intervals to find correlations between NBA playersâ€™ defensive statistics and perception of defensive skill

- Explored data using Matplotlib and Seaborn with visualizations like Multivariate Distributions

Interesting Findings

- Taller Players (Front Court): A good indicator of defensive prowess is not blocks but rather the FG% alowed within 5 ft.

- Shorter Players (Guards): A good indicator of good defense is actually Defensive Rating (the amount of points conceded per 100 possessions) of a team. This is interesting considering guards are normally considered the first line of defense for teams.
